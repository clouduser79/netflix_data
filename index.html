Finding 01 · Growth Divergence
TV Show additions grew at a CAGR of 20.6% vs Movies at just 5.5% (2010–2021). TV growth is ~4× faster.
Finding 02 · Crossover Event
Movies peaked at 767 titles in 2017, then declined sharply. TV Shows peaked at 436 in 2020 and overtook Movies in 2021.
Finding 03 · Ratio Compression
In 2010, there were 0.26 TV Shows per Movie. By 2021, that ratio had risen to 1.14 — a complete inversion in a decade.

YES — TV Shows Trend Higher
TV Show additions grew 3.7× faster than Movies over the 2010–2021 period. With a CAGR of 20.6% vs 5.5%, and a ratio that flipped from 0.26 to 1.14, the evidence is unambiguous.
